About Me

Full stack web developer with a background in finance. I graduated recently from the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp at Rutgers University. Growing up, I played organized sports in basketball and baseball with interests in gaming. I received an academic scholarship to Northwood University located in West Palm Beach, FL where I went to obtain my Bachelors of Business Administration and majoring in Finance. My first job I worked for a broker dealer firm, setting up 403b's, mutual funds, and annuities for clients. I then went onto doing some accounts receivable work for a hospitality management corporation. After that, I did a lot of customer service as a front desk admin for a manufacturing company. After working those jobs I felt like I needed a change and wanted to relocate back home to NJ in hopes of better opportunity. So far, I've been working for a real estate company, working commissions basically ensuring all agents who sell properties across the Tri-State area, Pennsyvlania, and Connecticut get cut the right commission checks. Currently, I reside in Jersey City, NJ where I aspire to begin a new career in the tech industry as a web developer/software engineer.
